### STEP 6 — LinkedIn Algorithm Rules (Avoid LinkedIn Jail)
**Status:** Reference — read before every post

**What gets you suppressed:**
- External links IN the post body (not comments — comments are fine)
- Engagement bait: "Comment YES if you agree" — LinkedIn detects and penalizes this
- Posting and ghosting — not replying to comments kills reach fast
- Buying followers or using engagement pods — algorithm detects coordinated behavior
- Reposting the exact same content too close together

**What boosts reach:**
- Asking a genuine question at the end (not bait — real question)
- Replying to every comment, especially in first 60 minutes
- Dwell time — carousels/documents people swipe through signal quality
- Posting consistently from the same account on the same topics
- First comment with your link (does NOT penalize reach)

**Safe link placement:**
✅ First comment immediately after posting
✅ In your LinkedIn profile bio
✅ In your "Featured" section on your profile
❌ In the post body itself
